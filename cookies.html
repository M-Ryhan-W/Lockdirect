Cookie Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026

The short version

This site keeps cookie use to an absolute minimum. We don't track you with advertising cookies. We don't build profiles. We don't sell your data to anyone.

If all you want to know: we use essential cookies needed for the site to function (none collect personal data), and we load a small number of third-party resources (Google Fonts and OpenStreetMap embed) which set their own cookies under their own terms.

What's a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that a website asks your browser to store on your device. It can be used to remember you between visits, keep you signed in, or track usage. There are also "local storage" entries (a similar concept used by modern web apps).

Managing cookies

You can manage and delete cookies through your browser's settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Blocking all third-party cookies and resources should not break this site's core function (you can still submit the dispatch form and call us), but maps and fonts may not display.

Changes to this policy

If we add any features that change how cookies are used, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date at the top.
