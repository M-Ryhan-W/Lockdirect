FAQ

What everyone asks first.

How much will it cost?

House lockouts from £79. Car lockouts from £89. Lock changes from £99. You'll get a fixed quote before any work starts — no surprise charges, no "while we're here" upsells. We never charge a call-out fee on top.

How fast can you actually get to me?

Our live average across Greater Manchester is 30–60 minutes. City centre and inner suburbs (M1, M4, M14, M20, M21, Salford, Stockport) are usually closer to 30 minutes. Outer boroughs (Wigan, Bolton, Rochdale) can be 45–75 mins depending on time of day.

Are you really open 24/7?

Yes. Our dispatch line answers around the clock — Christmas Day, 3am Sunday, the lot. Night callouts are the same price as daytime within standard service hours; emergency premium only applies to specialist out-of-hours commercial jobs.

Will you damage my door?

Wherever possible, we use non-destructive entry — picking, bypassing, or rekeying. That's why we send trained locksmiths, not handymen. If a lock has to be drilled (rare — usually only on high-security or already-broken locks), you'll be told and quoted before we touch it.

Can I trust the person you send?

Every locksmith in our network is DBS-checked, identity-verified, and fully insured. They carry photo ID. If you're ever unsure, ask to see it before you let them in — a real locksmith will always show it without hesitation.

What if I'm not 100% sure I'm locked out yet?

Call us anyway. The advice is free. We often talk people through checking the obvious things first — second door, window, key still in the lock from inside — before we dispatch. We'd rather lose a callout than charge you for one you didn't need.

What payment do you take?

Card (contactless / chip & PIN), cash, or bank transfer. Payment is taken on completion, never up-front. VAT receipts available on request — useful if you're claiming through home insurance.

Do you handle insurance claims?

Yes. We provide itemised invoices and incident reports suitable for most UK home insurance claims, particularly burglary repairs. If your insurer needs to authorise the work first, just let our dispatcher know on the call.
