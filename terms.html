2. What we do

LockDirect is a dispatch service. We connect customers needing emergency or scheduled locksmith work with vetted, DBS-checked locksmiths operating across Greater Manchester. We are not a single locksmith — we coordinate a network of independent specialists.

This distinction matters: the locksmith who arrives at your address is your direct contractor for the work itself. LockDirect is responsible for vetting and dispatching them, and we stand behind the 12-month workmanship guarantee.

3. Pricing and quotes

We provide indicative pricing on our website (house lockouts from £79, car lockouts from £89, lock changes from £99). The exact price for your specific job will be quoted to you on the phone before we dispatch an engineer.

The quote you accept on the phone is the price you pay. We never charge a call-out fee on top of the agreed price, and we do not add "while we're here" upsells without a clear, separate agreement.

If on arrival the engineer finds the job is materially different from what was described (e.g. you described a standard cylinder lockout but the door has been damaged in a forced entry attempt), they will give you a revised quote in writing before doing further work. You're free to decline.
